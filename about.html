About Based Users

We're bridging the gap between AI companies and real users, creating a platform where innovation meets authentic feedback.

Our Story

Born from the frustration of building AI products without real user insights

In 2023, our founders were building an AI startup and struggling with a critical problem: how do you get meaningful feedback from real AI users without spending months and thousands of dollars on traditional user research?

After paying $15,000 for a 6-week user research study that delivered generic insights from non-AI users, we realized the market was broken. AI companies needed fast, authentic feedback from people who actually understand and use AI tools daily.

The Breakthrough Moment

We discovered there were thousands of AI enthusiasts and professionals who would love to test new products and earn money doing it. The problem wasn't supply or demand‚Äîit was connection.

Based Users was born to solve this exact problem. We created a platform that connects AI companies with verified, experienced AI users for rapid, high-quality feedback. No lengthy contracts, no middleman markups, just direct connection between builders and users.

Today, we've facilitated over 10,000 research sessions, helped 100+ AI companies make better product decisions, and enabled thousands of AI enthusiasts to earn money sharing their expertise.

Our Mission

To democratize AI user research by making it fast, affordable, and accessible to every AI company, regardless of size or budget.

We believe that better AI products come from understanding real user needs, not assumptions. Our platform ensures that every AI company can access the insights they need to build products people actually want to use.

  • Make user research accessible to all AI companies
  • Connect builders directly with real AI users
  • Deliver actionable insights in hours, not months
  • Create earning opportunities for AI enthusiasts
