Information We Collect

At Based Users, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our user research platform.

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, professional background, and AI tool experience when you register as a user or client
  • Contact Details: Phone number, company information, and communication preferences
  • Profile Data: Skills, experience level, industry background, and areas of interest
  • Verification Information: LinkedIn profiles, portfolio links, and professional credentials

Research and Usage Data

  • Research Responses: Feedback, answers, opinions, and insights provided during research sessions
  • Session Data: Participation history, session duration, and engagement metrics
  • Platform Usage: How you navigate and interact with our website and services
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and device specifications for security and optimization

Payment Information

  • Billing Details: Payment information processed securely through Stripe and PayPal
  • Transaction History: Records of payments made and received through our platform
  • Tax Information: Details required for tax reporting and compliance

How We Use Your Information

Platform Operations

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match users with relevant research opportunities
  • Facilitate communication between clients and research participants
  • Process payments to research participants and from clients
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries

Research and Analytics

  • Compile anonymized research insights for clients
  • Analyze platform usage to improve our services
  • Generate aggregate statistics and industry reports
  • Ensure the quality and authenticity of research participants

Communication

  • Send notifications about research opportunities
  • Provide updates about platform features and improvements
  • Share relevant industry insights and educational content
  • Communicate important policy changes or security updates

Information Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We only share data in the following circumstances:

With Clients (Anonymized)

  • Research insights and feedback in anonymized, aggregated form
  • General participant demographics without identifying information
  • Quality metrics and engagement statistics

With Service Providers

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) for financial transactions
  • Email service providers for communication
  • Cloud hosting providers for data storage and platform operation
  • Analytics tools for platform improvement (with anonymized data)

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law, court order, or government request
  • To protect our legal rights, prevent fraud, or ensure platform security
  • In connection with a business transfer or acquisition (with user notification)

Research Data Handling

Data Collection During Research

When you participate in research sessions, we collect:

  • Your responses to research questions and prompts
  • Feedback about products, features, and user experiences
  • Screen recordings or session recordings (with explicit consent)
  • Interaction data and usage patterns during testing

Data Processing and Anonymization

  • All research data is anonymized before sharing with clients
  • Personal identifiers are removed from research reports
  • Aggregated insights are created to protect individual privacy
  • Raw research data is securely stored and access is strictly limited

Data Retention

  • Research data is retained for the duration needed to complete projects
  • Anonymized insights may be retained for platform improvement
  • Personal research data is deleted upon account closure or request
  • Session recordings are deleted after research reports are completed

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of research opportunities at any time
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request data portability to another service

Communication Preferences

  • Unsubscribe from research opportunity emails
  • Adjust notification preferences in your account settings
  • Choose which types of research you're interested in
  • Control the frequency of communications

Data Security

  • We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure data transmission and storage practices
  • Limited access controls and employee training on data privacy

Privacy Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your privacy rights, please contact our privacy team.
