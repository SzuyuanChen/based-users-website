Based Users is a platform that connects AI companies with verified users for research purposes. We facilitate user research sessions, collect feedback, and provide insights to our clients.

  • Service quality may vary based on research complexity and requirements

Platform Access

  • Account sharing is prohibited

Refund Policy

Client Refunds

Clients can request full refunds within 7 days if:

  • Research quality doesn't meet our agreed standards
  • We fail to deliver research results within promised timeframes
  • Technical issues prevent successful research completion
  • We cannot provide the required number of qualified participants

Refund Process

  • Refund requests must be submitted in writing with detailed reasoning
  • We will investigate all refund requests within 5 business days
  • Approved refunds are processed within 10 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method

No Refund Situations

  • Research completed according to agreed specifications
  • Client dissatisfaction with legitimate user feedback
  • Changes in research requirements after project initiation
  • Requests made more than 30 days after service completion
